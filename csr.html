CSR

Corporate Social Responsibility

With Chinland Agriculture Product Company's sponsorship, Chin Business Association (Kalay) member provided training on how to plant Sacha Inchi. There were about 130 rural villagers attending the training.

To preserve Chin literature, Chinland Agriculture Product Company sponsored education for 200 children in 2017. The company also provided stationaries and gifts for lecturers. In addition, the company provided food and other aids to senior adults older than 70 years of age.


Chinland Agriculture Product Company donated 10 water pumps to 10 households in January 2018 at Kanan village where water is scarce.
