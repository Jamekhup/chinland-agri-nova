Solar Dryer

Benefits of Solar Dryer:


  1. The higher temperature, movement of the air and lower humidity decrease drying time and reduces the risk of spoilage by micro-organisms.
  2. Food is enclosed in the dryer and therefore protected from dust, insects, birds and animals.
  3. The dryers are water-proof, therefore, the food does not need to be moved during raining.
  4. They are dried over stainless steel mesh, rather plastic mesh that emits harmful gases at higher temperatures.
  5. Solar Dryer retains taste, colour, aroma and nutrients of food product. Very easy to use.


Why would someone buy our solar dryer?


  • Lower cost
  • Folding design (lower transportation cost)
  • Usable with sensitive products like apple, tomatoes, mango, jackfruit etc. which cannot be dried using polyhouse dryer or open sun drying.
  • Provides the best quality products
  • Takes less drying time
  • Portable (can be moved in case of emergency)
  • Modular design
  • Automatic Temperature Control System
